Labi Wine

"LabiWine" winery is dedicated to the art of wine quality. Our vineyards are planted in the best soils and hills exposed to the sun, worked with passion and love. The grapes are harvested by hand, while the processing is done with small machines, enabling the selection of the highest quality grapes. This is us and our name will be a symbol of high quality wine.

Labi Wine

The end of the war in Kosovo in 1999 created the possibility to start cultivating and we preserve our centuries-old tradition. We continued to cultivate according to tradition, producing and selling quality wine, but without bren. The State of Kosovo has created new opportunities, opening perspectives bigger for grape growers and local wine producers. We started the work with a few primitive tools and with a destroyed family economy.
